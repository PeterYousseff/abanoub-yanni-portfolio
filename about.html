About Abanoub

Abanob Yanni... holds a Bachelor's degree in Music Education with honors. began learning the piano at the age of 10 and started teaching piano to people of various ages at the age of 16.

He learned Eastern singing through his studies with Professor Dr. Mohammed Ashraf at the Faculty of Music Education at Helwan University. Later, he learned Western singing with Professor Gabriella Boda at Debrecen University in Hungary. He also studied choral conducting with the opera singer and maestro Yannis ibn Abdullah

He further honed his musical skills by studying music composition at the Conservatoire of Music through listening lectures.

Abanob Yanni is an Egyptian composer who has received several awards and certificates of appreciation for creating music for numerous plays and narrative films. Some of his notable works include the film "Kotar's Illusion" , the film "Lavender", and more recently, the film "Jewel's Legacy" in 2023.

Abanob Yanni is also the founder of Yanni Studio for recording, filming, and content creation in general.

He is interested in studying psychology, cognitive-behavioral therapy, behavior modification, and activities through his work at the Mental Health Hospital in Helwan, integrating artistic therapy with psychology.

Abanob Yanni has taught a large number of students in various fields and workshops in both artistic and psychological domains, both within and outside of Egypt.
