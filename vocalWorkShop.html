Whether you're a passionate singer, aspiring voice actor, or simply looking to improve your speaking voice, Abanoub's workshops have something to offer you.

Explore a wide range of vocal techniques:

Arabic Singing : Master the art of traditional Arabic singing, including maqamat (melodic modes) and improvisation.

Western Singing : Develop your vocal skills in a variety of Western styles, from classical to pop to jazz.

Voice Acting : Learn the techniques of voice acting for animation, video games, commercials, and more.

Dubbing : Discover the art of dubbing and learn how to bring characters to life in foreign language films and TV shows.

Abanoub's workshops are designed for all levels, from beginners to experienced vocalists.

His engaging and supportive teaching style will help you :

Develop your vocal range and power

Improve your pitch and tone

Learn how to breathe properly for singing

Overcome performance anxiety

Build confidence in your voice
